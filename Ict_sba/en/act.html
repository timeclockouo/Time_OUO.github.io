Three-Day Trip to Shenzhen

This study abroad trip allowed us to explore the diverse features of different places in Shenzhen and gain insights into the local tourism industry. The culture of Shenzhen truly opened our eyes.


On the first day, we took the Shenzhen Metro near the border area. Starting from Hong Kong, we took the East Rail Line of the MTR to Lo Wu Station, and then transferred to the Shenzhen Metro Luo Bao Line to Futian Checkpoint. Along the way, we encountered the three major transportation hubs of the Shenzhen Metro, gaining an understanding of the modern transportation network in Shenzhen.


On the second day, we visited different parts of Shenzhen and experienced various cultural aspects, witnessing the city's prosperity and distinctive road designs that set it apart from Hong Kong.


On the third day, we went to Dapeng Bay in Shenzhen, exploring the local culture and natural ecological environment, and discovering many fascinating stories along the way.

Two-Day Trip to Shanghai

Shanghai is a city with rich history and culture. The Shanghai Museum exposed us to various cultural aspects and broadened our horizons. Additionally, Zhuhai is also a beautiful city that is definitely worth a visit.


On the first day, we visited the Bund in Shanghai, where we learned about the diverse culture and characteristics of Zhuhai, gaining valuable insights.


On the second day, we visited the Yu Garden in Shanghai, where we learned about different historical aspects related to Zhuhai. We also appreciated the ancient architecture of Shanghai, further deepening our understanding of Shanghai's culture.
